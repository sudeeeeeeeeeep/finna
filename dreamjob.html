2. How to Write a Job-Winning Resume

Your resume is your first impression. Here’s how to make it stand out:

  • Use a clean, professional format with clear headings.
  • Include keywords from job descriptions to pass ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems).
  • Highlight skills, experience, and achievements.

3. Top Interview Tips to Get Hired Fast

Mastering interviews is key to landing your dream job. Follow these expert tips:

  • Research the company and understand its values.
  • Practice answering common interview questions.
  • Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to structure your answers.

4. The Role of AI in Modern Job Searches

AI is changing how companies hire. Here’s how it affects you:

  • AI-powered job matching with platforms like Finna.
  • Resume screening tools for automated shortlisting.
  • Virtual interviews & chatbots for faster hiring.

5. How to Build a Strong Professional Network

Networking is still one of the most powerful ways to find a job. Follow these tips:

  • Optimize your LinkedIn profile with a professional photo and detailed bio.
  • Engage with industry leaders by commenting on their posts.
  • Join online communities and attend virtual job fairs.
